How I started Skateboarding

It started when I was 13 years old, and my sisters boyfriend at the time gave me a World Industries skateboard.

Was it easy?

In the begining it wasn't easy but throughout the years it became easier to learn some tricks.

Do you enjoy it?

Skateboarding has been an amazing thing that happened to me. It taught me to never give up, and no matter the circumstance you can achieve as long as you put the effort.
